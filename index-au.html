What Are Clear Aligners?

Clear aligners are a modern, virtually invisible alternative to traditional metal braces. These custom-made, removable trays gently shift your teeth into the desired position over time, providing you with a comfortable and discreet way to achieve a perfect smile. With Dr Clear Aligners, you can straighten your teeth without the hassle and discomfort of traditional braces.

Why Choose Clear Aligners?

Here are 4 major reasons why clear aligners could be the perfect choice for you:

Discreet

Clear aligners are virtually invisible, making them a more discreet option compared to metal braces. They are made from smooth plastic, offering a comfortable fit with no painful wires or brackets.

Removable

Clear aligners are removable, so you can easily take them out when eating, drinking, brushing, and flossing. This makes maintaining oral hygiene much easier compared to traditional braces.

Faster

Many patients experience faster treatment times with clear aligners compared to metal braces, with the added benefit of fewer check-ups required throughout the treatment process.

Customised

Using advanced 3D imaging technology, Dr Clear Aligners creates a personalised treatment plan that targets your unique needs, ensuring the most effective results.

What Can Clear Aligners Fix?

Clear aligners are an effective solution for a wide range of misaligned teeth issues, including:

Crossbite

When teeth are misaligned, causing them to bite incorrectly

Underbite

When the lower teeth overlap the upper teeth

Teeth Gap

Gaps between teeth that may affect the alignment

Overbite

When the upper teeth significantly overlap the lower teeth

Open Bite

When the upper and lower teeth do not meet

Crowded Teeth

When teeth overlap or are out of position due to lack of space

No matter your teeth alignment issue, Dr Clear Aligners can help you find a tailored solution to get your smile back on track.
